LEGWORK capabilities

What can (and can't!) LEGWORK do?

LEGWORK can:

  • Calculate gravitational-wave strains for any harmonic
  • Evolve the orbits of sources
  • Compute signal-to-noise ratios in a variety of detectors
  • Visualise the results

LEGWORK implements post-Newtonian approximations for its calculations...it can only be used for sources where these approximations are valid!
