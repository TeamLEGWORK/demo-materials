A brief note on software citation

Citing software consistently and uniformly is important

Software has not always been consistently cited - despite its importance to strengthen support for software development

(Howison & Bullard 2016; Niemeyer et al. 2016; Li et al. 2017; Bouquin et al. 2020; Alsudais 2021; Bouquin et al. 2023).
