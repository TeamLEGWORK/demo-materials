Open-source

The open-source nature of LEGWORK promotes collaboration on a centralised tool

Reduces barrier to entry in the field and avoids mistakes from re-deriving and implementing the equations

Efficient

LEGWORK is fully vectorised and easily scales to large populations on a galactic scale

SNR and eccentricity approximations are applied dynamically based on user-tuned tolerances
